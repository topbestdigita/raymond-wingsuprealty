CONNECTIVITY

  • EXISTING
  • UPCOMING

Located in Central Thane and extremely well connected with Mumbai through Eastern Freeway & Eastern Expressway to South & Central Mumbai, and through GB road to Western Mumbai.
Work in progress on new flyovers connecting Ghodbunder Road with Dahisar and Bhiwandi, to make the commute even easier.
The recent opening of the final arm called Kapurbawdi flyover has made commuting easier to Mumbai.
Internal transportation is getting better with initiatives like the SATIS (Station Area Traffic Improvement Scheme).

The state government is planning to construct a tunnel linking Thane with Borivali, which will further shorten the travelling distance to the Western suburbs to a 10 min drive â€“ State Wildlife Board gives clearance for Thane-Borivali tunnel survey.
Thane will soon be one of the few cities in India to have metro connectivity! Mumbai Metro has recently crystallized plans for Thane and the Wadala-Ghatkopar metro line that will be extended to Teen Haath Naka &Kasarwadavali near GB Road.
Large proposed infrastructure development including tourism and water projects.
The Thane Municipal Corporation (TMC) will soon introduce an elevated mode of rapid public transport (RPT) to operate within the city.
There are advanced talks being held between MMRDA and various infrastructural consultants to bring the Monorail to Thane as well, with the Thane-Bhiwandi-Kalyan Monorail Corridor.
